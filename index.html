Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

All financial data entered into BudgetFlow is stored securely and is used only to provide budgeting and analytics features within the app.

We use secure cloud services such as Firebase to store application data. We take reasonable measures to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your information is shared only with trusted services required to operate the app (e.g., analytics and cloud storage providers).

BudgetFlow is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
